SoundCloud depicts audio tracks graphically as waveforms and allows users to post "timed comments" on specific parts of any track. These comments are displayed while the associated audio segment is played. Users are allowed to create playlists (previously known as "sets"), and to "Like" (specific tracks, which will then be saved to the user's "Like" page), "Repost", "Share", to "Follow" another user, and to make complimentary downloads of their audio available. SoundCloud's API allows programs to upload music and sound files, or download files if the uploader has gave permission to do so.This API has been integrated into several applications, including DAW software such as GarageBand, Logic Pro, and Studio One.
